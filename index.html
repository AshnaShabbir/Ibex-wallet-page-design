Key Features

control of your spending

Budgets help you cut down spending on unwanted categories, so you have more for the things you really need. Setup spending limits on categories that you want to monitor closely. As new transactions are automatically synced from your bank, you will receive real-time progress and alerts on how you are doing.

Preview your future

Forecast estimates your future spending, income and account balances based on your past spending, upcoming transactions, reminders and budgets. This feature is extremely useful for planning for near-term goals and making sure you have sufficient funds in the right accounts.

Retirement Planner

Retirement Planner will forecast your net worth over time, taking into account factors such as inflation, investment returns, and taxes. Your financial plan will let you know if you are on track for retirement and help you make big decision.This decision is often influenced by a combination of your lifestyle.

Learn Financial Management with Ibex Wallet

At Ibex Wallet, we believe that effective financial management is key to achieving your financial goals. Whether you're new to managing cryptocurrency or looking to optimize your investments, our app provides an intuitive platform to help you track, organize, and grow your assets. With features like real-time portfolio tracking, spending analytics, and automated budget planning, Ibex Wallet empowers you to take control of your financial journey.
